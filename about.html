A Little About Me

My name is Emma Wang. I am a visual and UX designer currently working at LingoLeap.ai.

Hi, I'm Emma Wang, a visual and product designer with a passion for crafting digital experiences that are as intuitive as they are beautiful. Currently, I'm designing the future of language learning at LingoLeap.ai, where I get to blend creativity with purpose every day.

I'm a self-driven designer who thrives on solving complex problems with simplicity and elegance. Figma is my playground, and I love turning ideas into pixel-perfect interfaces that users can't help but connect with. Originally from the lush, green landscapes of Zhejiang, China, I now find inspiration in the foggy charm of San Francisco, where I've built my career and honed my craft.

When I'm not designing, you'll probably find me browsing local plant stores, admiring window cats and window plants. Let's connect and create something meaningful together!
