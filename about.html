A Little About Me

My name is Emma Wang. I am a visual and UX designer currently working at LingoLeap.ai.

I'm a responsible and self-driven designer who loves using Figma to craft simple yet visually engaging digital interfaces. Originally from China's lush Zhejiang province, I now call foggy SF home. My design journey began at San Francisco State University, where I honed my skills in UX, graphic design, and interactive design.

To me, designing solutions is a profound responsibility that demands an intimate understanding of users' aspirations. I believe in leading with open-mindedness and empathy, valuing the perspectives of others to create thoughtful designs.
